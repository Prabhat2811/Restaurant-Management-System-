FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I add my restaurant to Savor? ▾
Register with the Restaurant Admin role, then use the Add Restaurant form. Your restaurant goes live immediately and you can start adding menu categories and items right away.
Is there a free trial available? ▾
Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free forever with up to 50 menu items. The Pro plan comes with a 30-day free trial — no credit card required.
What payment methods are supported? ▾
We support UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery. Payment status is automatically tracked and synced with every order.
Can I manage multiple branches? ▾
Multi-branch management is available on the Enterprise plan. Each branch gets its own menu, orders, and delivery agents while sharing a central admin dashboard.
How does the delivery agent system work? ▾
Register delivery agents with their vehicle numbers. When an order is ready, assign an available agent. The system prevents assignment of unavailable agents automatically. The agent's availability updates after each delivery.
